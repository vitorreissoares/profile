Vitor Reis Soares

PhD student in Political Science at the Institute of Social and Political Studies, Rio de Janeiro State University (IESP-UERJ). Author of the first study conducted in Brazil to be published in the Journal of Political Marketing, in which he defined the concept of Political Human Branding. Data Analyst for Public Policy at Rio de Janeiro State University (UERJ). Research interests include Political Marketing, Political Communication, Political Branding, and the monitoring and evaluation of elections and public policies using Data Science (R and Python).
